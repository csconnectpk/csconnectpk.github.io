002 / FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CS Connect really free?

Yes, 100% free. CS Connect is a registered nonprofit. We don't charge for membership, resources, or any of our services. We believe education and mentorship should be accessible to everyone.

Who can join CS Connect?

Anyone interested in computer science, whether you're a pre-university student preparing for entry tests, a current CS undergraduate, a graduate student, or even a working professional wanting to give back. We welcome students from all universities and cities across Pakistan.

Do I need to be a CS student to join?

Not necessarily. While our primary audience is CS students, we welcome anyone passionate about technology and computer science. Many of our members come from related fields like IT, Software Engineering, Data Science, and Electrical Engineering.

What's the difference between WhatsApp and Discord?

WhatsApp is our primary community. It's where most conversations happen, announcements are made, and quick discussions take place. Discord offers more structured channels, voice chat rooms, and is great for longer technical discussions and study sessions. We recommend joining both.

How can I contribute to CS Connect?

There are many ways to contribute: share your entry test experience, create study resources, mentor junior students, volunteer for events, or help with content creation. Reach out to any admin in the community and we'll find the right fit for you.

Can I be a podcast guest?

Absolutely! If you have a unique story or experience in the CS field, whether it's about your entry test journey, university life, career path, or side projects, we'd love to have you. Contact us through WhatsApp or Discord to pitch your story.

Is there any spam in the groups?

We have strict no-spam policies and active moderators. Our community is focused on genuine discussions, resource sharing, and helping each other. Any promotional or spam content is removed immediately.
