About CS Connect

Students helping students get better at CS and find their way in tech.

001 / Our Story

How It All Started

It started at FAST Islamabad. A few CS students noticed something that bothered them: students at well-known universities had access to seniors in FAANG, alumni networks, career guidance, and a culture of building things. Students at most other universities across Pakistan had none of that. Same degree, completely different experience.

So a group of us started a WhatsApp group. Just 30 people sharing entry test tips and career advice. No grand plan. But it grew fast because the need was real. Within months, we had students from 50+ universities joining, asking questions, helping each other prep for tests, sharing internship leads, and actually building a community that worked.

Today CS Connect is a registered nonprofit with 3000+ members across 60+ universities. We run a podcast, organize workshops, and maintain free resources. But the core idea hasn't changed: every CS student in Pakistan should have access to the same mentorship and opportunities, regardless of which university they ended up at.
